**TAKEAWAYS**
- There is a <Underlining color={Theme.colors.secondary} hoverColor={Theme.colors.secondary}>connection between the standard of the technical equipment and the expectations</Underlining>, regarding the different types of school and the individual teachers, students, and media officers.

- Teachers tend to have <Underlining color={Theme.colors.secondary} hoverColor={Theme.colors.secondary}>a misconception of the media competencies</Underlining> of their students and to overevaluate them.

- Students and teachers often have very <Underlining color={Theme.colors.secondary} hoverColor={Theme.colors.secondary}>different attributions of value</Underlining> of digital media. 

- Generally, all interviewees advocated an <Underlining color={Theme.colors.secondary} hoverColor={Theme.colors.secondary}>increased use of media in the future</Underlining>, despite the current compromised realities.
